What is Crate Digger?

Crate Digger treats Spotify as your music shopping list. If you use Spotify to aggregate tracks you discover through movies, radio, friends, Shazam, etc., you can use Crate Digger to cross-reference Spotify against your local library so you know what to buy vs. what you already own. For tracks you don't own yet, Crate Digger provides one‑click links out to purchase sources (Amazon, Bandcamp, Beatport, etc.).

1Connect Spotify
Browse your playlists inside Crate Digger. Track counts and cover art included.
2Connect your local library
Crate Digger scans your library so it can tell you what tracks you already own.
3Buy what you’re missing
Quickly link out to your favorite music store to buy the tracks you’re missing.
